Organic Meal Prep in Orange County

Primal Plates is Orange County's source for organic, seed oil free meal prep. Every meal is made from scratch using pasture raised meats, grass fed beef, and ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers like Lundberg Farms.

We deliver weekly throughout OC, from Huntington Beach and Newport Beach along the coast to inland cities like Irvine, Anaheim, and Mission Viejo. Whether you're in South County near Laguna Beach and Dana Point, or North County in Fullerton and Yorba Linda, we've got you covered.

Our weekly rotating menu features dishes like slow-cooked barbacoa bowls, Mediterranean chicken with coconut rice, grass fed picanha with bourbon butter, and more. Everything is cooked with real fats -- butter, olive oil, coconut oil, and tallow. No canola, no soybean oil, no shortcuts.

Order by Sunday, get fresh meals delivered during the week. It's that simple.
